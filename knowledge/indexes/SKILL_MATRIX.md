## Level Calibration

- **Beginner:** explains and follows guidance.
- **Intermediate:** implements independently in bounded scope.
- **Senior:** owns production quality and failure handling.
- **Staff:** creates leverage across systems and teams.
- **Principal:** shapes strategy and resolves organization-wide ambiguity.
- **Architect:** integrates product, organization, data, platform, risk, and
  long-term evolution.
