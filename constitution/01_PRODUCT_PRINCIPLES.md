# MarketMind AI Product Principles

## 1. Evidence Over Opinion
Every insight, summary, recommendation, or alert must be backed by verifiable data, documents, or calculations.

## 2. AI Assists, Humans Decide
MarketMind AI provides decision support. It must never claim guaranteed returns or make final investment decisions for the user.

## 3. Official Data First
Prefer official sources such as NSE, BSE, SEBI, RBI, company investor relations pages, annual reports, and quarterly filings.

## 4. Transparency by Default
Every AI-generated answer should explain why it reached a conclusion and show the source documents or metrics used.

## 5. Trust Before Speed
Fast answers are useful, but trusted answers are mandatory. Accuracy, traceability, and auditability matter more than speed.

## 6. Research Before Recommendation
The platform should analyze company documents, financials, valuation, risks, portfolio fit, and market context before suggesting any action.

## 7. Personalization With Control
The system should adapt to the user's portfolio, risk profile, investment horizon, and strategy, but the user should always remain in control.

## 8. Long-Term Thinking
The product should encourage disciplined investing, risk management, and evidence-based decisions rather than speculation or hype.

## 9. Privacy First
User portfolio data, strategy, and personal financial information must be protected by default.

## 10. Build for Daily Use
The product should be useful every morning: market brief, portfolio status, key alerts, new filings, risks, and opportunities.
