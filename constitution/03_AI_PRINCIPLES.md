# MarketMind AI Principles

## 1. Retrieval Before Generation
The AI must retrieve relevant evidence before generating an answer.

## 2. Evidence Based Responses
Every recommendation should reference the documents or market data used.

## 3. Confidence Score
Every AI response should expose a confidence level.

## 4. Explainability
Users should understand why the AI produced a recommendation.

## 5. No Hallucinations
When sufficient evidence is unavailable, the AI should explicitly state that it cannot determine an answer.

## 6. Human Oversight
AI recommendations always require human judgment before execution.

## 7. Multi-Agent Collaboration
Specialized AI agents collaborate to produce higher quality decisions.

## 8. Continuous Learning
The platform continuously indexes newly available official documents and market information.

## 9. Local First
Whenever practical, inference and embeddings should run locally.

## 10. Responsible AI
MarketMind AI never guarantees investment returns or financial outcomes.
